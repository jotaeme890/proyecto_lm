RULES to be followed in the Tournaments
- The total number of participating couples will be approximately 8 to 12, the start time will be 9.00 am approx., the end time is estimated around 23.00 pm.

Location
- The Tournament will take place on 10 September 2022, in the facilities of the Ciudad Deportiva Carranque.

Rules
- They will play against each other.

- The duration of the matches will be 1 hour, approx.

- The matches will be played to 2 sets, if it is appreciated that there will not be time to start playing the tiebreaker set (2nd), an agreement will be reached between the couples on how to play it, otherwise, it will be decided by the Organization.

- If a match ends in a draw, the winner will be the pair with the most games won.

- Matches won will score 1 point, matches lost will score 0 points.

- The winner of the Tournament will be the one who has won the most games during this group. If there is a tie, it will be decided by the number of games won by each pair in the matches played.

- The official tables with the matches and schedules will be published on this website, being the responsibility of each participant to consult them.

Timetables
- The first match will be at 9.00 am on Saturday.

Installations
- It will be played on outdoor artificial turf courts. Wall and Glass

Player substitutions
- One member of the couple may be substituted in the following cases:
- Indefinite injury.

- For a major cause (duly justified), which prevents him/her from participating in the Tournament.

- No-show, withdrawal or expulsion from the competition

- Failure to appear on court 5 minutes after the start time of the match will automatically result in the forfeit of the match by a score of two sets to zero. If you have two "no-shows" you will be expelled from the competition.

- If a couple withdraws or is expelled from the Tournament, all the results obtained by this couple during the competition will be annulled, giving the match won by 2 sets to 0 to the rivals, in each of the clashes.

- When a couple withdraws or is expelled from the competition, the amount paid will not be refunded in any case.

Injuries
- The Organisers cannot be held responsible for any injuries sustained during the course of the competition.

Cancellations
- The Organisation is not responsible for any type of cancellation of matches, acts or events, including total cancellation of the Tournament for justified reasons beyond the Organisation's control, due to weather conditions or any other cause, and is not obliged in any case to refund all or part of the registration fee.

Presentation of Trophies
- The trophy ceremony will take place on 10/09/2022 at approximately 19:00 hours at the tracks or on another day at a place assigned for this purpose.

Compliance with Standards
- All couples registered in the "Open Padel Amigos" agree to comply with the rules of the competition.

- Entries will under no circumstances be refunded in cash, they can be exchanged (in case they need to be refunded) for other entries to any of our Tournaments.

- The Organising Committee has the right to apply any sanction it deems appropriate to any situation that arises and is not specifically covered by these rules.

- The Organising Committee will be responsible for drawing up the draws and orders of play and for resolving any incidents that may arise during the matches, its decisions being final.

- The Organising Committee reserves the right to change any of these rules if necessary for the smooth running of the tournament.
